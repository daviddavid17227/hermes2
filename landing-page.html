Walkthrough

A giving loop designed for fans, not forms.

The product turns a standard donation into a repeatable, game-day engagement loop: give, receive credits, make picks, redeem donated rewards, then come back for the next campaign.

01

Choose a cause

Supporters pick a campaign: youth sports, school athletics, medical foundations, disaster relief, or team-backed nonprofits.

02

Donate for credits

Every eligible donation unlocks non-cash Sports Raise credits that create participation, not financial payout.

03

Play prediction contests

Fans use credits on sports-style picks, brackets, and game-day challenges that make giving feel active.

04

Redeem donated prizes

Credits and entries unlock donated memorabilia, sponsor goods, VIP experiences, and campaign-specific rewards.

Why it works

More excitement than a donate button. More purpose than a sportsbook.

Sports Raise borrows the engagement mechanics fans already understand, then points that attention toward fundraising, sponsor goodwill, and community outcomes.

For nonprofitsCampaign pages get a reason for supporters to return throughout a season, not just during one pledge drive.
For fansGiving feels participatory: credits, picks, leaderboards, drops, and rewards add a game layer to generosity.
For sponsorsBrands can donate prizes and experiences while being visibly connected to measurable community impact.
For teams & athletesMemorabilia and access become a structured way to mobilize fan bases for causes they care about.
