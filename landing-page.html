Questions operators ask

Clear answers before launch.

Who is Sports Raise built for first?

Campaign operators: nonprofits, charitable organizers, community fundraisers, and individuals who want a repeatable sports-driven way to activate supporters.

How do supporters participate after donating?

Supporters receive non-cash credits connected to the campaign, then use them for picks, predictions, and game-day challenges tied to donated rewards and leaderboards.

Where do rewards come from?

Rewards can come from campaign creators, local businesses, sponsors, teams, athletes, memorabilia donors, or starter items used for early campaigns.

Can a campaign launch before all game details are final?

Yes. Operators can launch the cause page, begin sharing, build the supporter list, and seed the prize pool before the first game-day challenge opens.

What makes this different from a normal donation page?

A normal page asks once. Sports Raise creates recurring moments: game schedules, weekly challenges, reward updates, leaderboards, and impact reports.
