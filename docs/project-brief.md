# Sports Raise Project Brief

## One-line concept

Sports Raise is a donation-first sports engagement platform that helps campaign operators turn game-day energy into repeat contributions for causes.

## Primary audience

V1 is for campaign operators:
- Nonprofits
- Charitable organizers
- Individuals raising money for a cause
- Community sports families
- Early pilot users in David's network

Secondary/future audience:
- Fan contributors who donate, receive non-cash credits, participate in picks/challenges, compete for donated rewards, and follow impact updates.

## Core loop

1. Campaign operator launches a campaign page.
2. Supporters donate to the campaign.
3. Supporters receive non-cash credits.
4. Supporters use credits for game-day picks/challenges.
5. Donated rewards/prize pool create excitement.
6. Impact updates bring the mission back to the center.
7. Sharing toolkit and video assets help the campaign spread.

## Strategic differentiator

Most fundraising pages are static. Sports Raise creates reasons for supporters to come back throughout a season by connecting donations to game-day participation, rewards, and visible impact.

## Public copy guardrails

Use:
- game-day action
- picks
- predictions
- challenges
- non-cash credits
- donated rewards
- prize pool
- leaderboards
- campaign play
- impact

Avoid:
- sportsbook
- wagering
- cash betting
- cash-out
- gambling-first framing

## Immediate goal

Create a credible, premium first impression and prepare the structure for early pilot campaigns, especially around football season.
