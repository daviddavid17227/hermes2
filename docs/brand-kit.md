# Sports Raise Brand Kit v0.1

> Working brand kit. This is not final identity design; it is the first durable direction so Hermes, Codex, and future designers stop improvising.

## Brand position

Sports Raise is a donation-first sports engagement platform for campaign operators. It helps nonprofits, charitable organizers, and individuals turn game-day energy into repeat contributions through non-cash credits, picks/challenges, donated rewards, sharing assets, and visible impact.

## Brand personality

- Premium sports-tech
- Trustworthy fintech
- Energetic but not juvenile
- Cause-first, not betting-first
- Cinematic, polished, launch-ready
- Practical for campaign managers

## Visual principles

1. **Cinematic first impression** — the hero should feel like a high-end product launch, not a template.
2. **Donation-first clarity** — every visual should support campaign creation, sharing, rewards, credits, and impact.
3. **Sports energy without gambling cues** — use fields, scoreboards, game-day language, picks/challenges; avoid odds boards, casino cues, betting slips, dice, chips, cash-out language.
4. **3D with purpose** — 3D visuals should explain the connected system, not decorate the page.
5. **Mobile credibility** — the brand cannot feel premium if the phone view breaks.

## Typography direction

Current production-safe default: Inter.

Desired feel:
- Big, tight, confident display headlines.
- Calm, readable body copy.
- Uppercase labels for product/section framing.
- Slight sports-broadcast/editorial energy.

Future type exploration:
- Continue with Inter for MVP.
- Explore a more distinctive display face for wordmark/headlines if needed.
- Avoid playful rounded startup fonts.

## Logo direction

The logo should eventually include:

1. **Wordmark:** “Sports Raise” in a confident, modern sans-serif.
2. **Icon mark:** A compact symbol that can work as favicon/app icon/social avatar.
3. **Campaign powered-by lockup:** “Powered by Sports Raise” for campaign pages where the campaign/cause is the star.

Potential icon concepts:
- **Route + field mark:** A glowing game path crossing a field/campaign platform.
- **SR monogram:** Interlocked S/R shaped like a route, ribbon, or stadium path.
- **Impact arc:** A rising line/arc from supporter energy to cause impact.
- **Credit-to-impact loop:** Circular loop showing donation → credits → picks → rewards → impact.

Avoid:
- Generic trophy-only logo.
- Gambling/casino marks.
- Literal sportsbook-style marks.
- Clipart footballs as the whole identity.

## Hero/visual system

The current direction is a premium 3D isometric ecosystem:
- Campaign page
- Donation + non-cash credits
- Game-day picks/challenges
- Sharing toolkit
- Donated rewards/prize pool
- Impact dashboard

Visual material language:
- Deep navy environment
- Frosted white/dark glass panels
- Electric blue route glows
- Emerald impact signals
- Gold reward highlights
- Soft global illumination
- Subtle film grain

## Voice and copy rules

Use:
- game-day action
- picks
- predictions
- challenges
- non-cash credits
- donated rewards
- prize pool
- leaderboards
- campaign play
- impact
- sharing toolkit
- campaign page
- AI video assets

Avoid public-facing use of:
- sportsbook
- wagering
- cash betting
- cash-out
- gambling-first framing

## Component rules

Buttons:
- Primary: action cyan/blue, pill, strong contrast.
- Secondary: dark/translucent surface, light text, subtle border.
- Mobile tap target: at least 44px high.

Cards:
- Dark product cards for cinematic sections.
- White cards for explanatory sections.
- Avoid repeated generic icon cards.

Campaign pages:
- Campaign/cause is the star.
- Sports Raise brand visible but secondary.
- Sharing tools must be prominent.
