# Sharing Toolkit Requirements

## Goal

Make it unbelievably easy for fundraisers, campaign managers, nonprofits, admins, employees, and supporters to share a campaign immediately.

## Toolkit assets

- Email template: organizer announcement
- Email template: supporter forward-to-friend
- SMS copy
- Facebook post
- Instagram caption
- Instagram story/reel prompt
- TikTok caption and video prompt
- LinkedIn post for nonprofit/admin audiences
- Trackable campaign links
- QR code
- Embeddable donate/share widget
- Sponsor/partner outreach blurb
- 15-second campaign video
- 30-second campaign video

## Product behavior

- Generate assets from campaign inputs.
- Let operator edit before publishing.
- One-click copy/download/share where possible.
- Track which channels drive visits/donations.
- Let supporters share their own version after donating.

## MVP approach

Start with templates and copy/download buttons. Add dynamic AI video generation after the campaign data model stabilizes.
